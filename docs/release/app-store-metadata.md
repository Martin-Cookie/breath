### Description (max 4000 chars)
```
Breath is an elegant app for guided breathing and breath-hold training. Inspired by the Wim Hof method, designed for daily practice.

HOW IT WORKS
Each session has three phases:
• Breathing — deep inhales and exhales in a calm rhythm
• Retention — hold with empty lungs, visually guided by a circle
• Recovery — short inhale and hold before the next round begins

KEY FEATURES
• Clean configuration — speed, rounds, breath count
• Visual guidance — breathing circles in a warm palette
• Audio guidance and background music — optional per phase
• Haptic feedback — subtle vibrations at key moments
• Statistics — every session saved, progress charts, streak
• Widget — your current streak right on the home screen
• Share results — brag to friends about your longest hold
• Daily reminders — a gentle nudge to keep your practice going
• Fully localized (Czech & English)

BREATH PRO
The free tier gives you everything you need to start. Breath Pro unlocks:
• Three breathing speeds (Standard / Fast / Slow)
• All music beds and guidance styles
• Unlimited history (free: 7 days)
• Home screen widget
• Share results

Pro is a one-time purchase — no subscription, no hidden fees.

PRIVACY
Breath collects no personal data. All sessions stay on your device. No ads. No tracking. No cloud servers.

HEALTH WARNING
Breath is not a medical device. Never hold your breath in water, while driving, or if you have cardiovascular, respiratory, or mental health conditions. Consult a doctor if in doubt.
```
